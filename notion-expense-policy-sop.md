**Expense Policy**

This Policy sets out the principles and rules around incurring expenses and budget deployment with the following main objectives:
- Responsible spending and focus on controlling
- Value optimization
- Provision of the most appropriate services / products, at the right time & quantity, always covering the tailored needs of internal stakeholders

---

## HR cost: Full time employees

For budget deployment relating to full-time employees, prospective employees shall be provided with an offer letter by Two's Head of Human Resources with employment terms in line with the terms accounted for as per the agreed Two's annual budget plan.

- If the proposed cost is higher and **up to 10%** of the accounted budget allocation, consent must be provided by Two's **CFO**
- If the proposed cost is higher than **20%** of the accounted budget allocation, consent must be provided by Two's **CEO**
- If the headcount is **not accounted for** in the Budget, consent must be provided by both Two's **CEO and CFO**

> **Note:** The policy does not specify what applies in the 10–20% range. This is a gap — do not assume either CFO or CEO approval applies.

## HR cost: Contractor spend

For budget deployment relating to 3rd party contractor spend, Two's Business Heads shall communicate corresponding needs to the Head of Human Resources, who will in turn proceed with drafting and finalizing respective employment terms in line with the budget allocations as per the agreed Two's annual budget plan.

- In the event the respective contractor spend is accounted for but the proposed cost is higher and **up to 20%** of the accounted budget allocation, consent must be provided by Two's **CFO**
- In the event the respective headcount is accounted for but the proposed cost is higher than **20%** of the accounted budget allocation, consent must be provided by Two's **CEO**
- In the event the respective headcount is **not accounted for** in the Budget, consent must be provided by both Two's **CEO and CFO**

## Expenses costs

Each of Two's business heads (c-level or functional head) should only approve expenses relevant to his/her business activity, that are within normal division/unit operations and according to the provisions of this Policy.

Each of Two's business heads (c-level or functional head) can approve the following expenses if not accounted as per the agreed Two's annual budget plan:
- **€300 EUR per invoice**
- **AND up to €1,000 EUR cumulative on a monthly basis**

Any excess of the limits mentioned above, either per one-off expense or per monthly cumulative limit, must be approved by the **CFO and the CEO**. Monthly expense summary per division to be prepared by Finance within 2 weeks from month end, to include itemized lists of all invoices approved.
