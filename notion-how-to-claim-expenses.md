# How to claim and report expenses

Source: https://app.notion.com/p/4d22b4190e654ba68d4f5140d4af56bc

---

Two expense reporting guidance covers two key processes:
1. Expenses incurred via company card
2. Expenses incurred via personal card

## Expenses incurred via company card

1. Ensure all reported expenses are in line with the Employee Expense Policy and all expenses (either personal or business) follow the principles detailed under the Expense Policy SOP.
   - ***If in doubt always check with your reporting line manager***
2. If you need to use the company card for any purchase please ensure you convey your purchase request to the Finance team (finance@two.inc) who will assess your request and approve as per internal policy.
3. Then the Two Finance Department will convey receipts and invoices to the accounting systems for appropriate book-keeping.

## Expenses incurred via personal card

1. Ensure all reported expenses are in line with the Employee Expense Policy and all expenses (either personal or business) follow the principles detailed under the Expense Policy SOP.
   - ***If in doubt always check with your reporting line manager***
2. Submit expenses using the correct app for your entity:
   - **Norway 🇳🇴** — download the **Tripletex** app. Issues: contact szifroni@two.inc
   - **UK 🇬🇧** — download the **Xero Me** app. Issues: contact szifroni@two.inc
   - **Sweden 🇸🇪** — download the **Fortnox** app. Issues: contact szifroni@two.inc
   - **ROW** — go to Two's Expense Filing report page in Notion.
3. Report your expense including all required fields:
   1. If an invoice, report the relevant due date.
   2. Ensure receipt or invoice is filed (payment receipt is not sufficient; document needs to show **what was bought**).
   3. For **travel expenses**, include in the Cost Commentary:
      1. Date and time of departure and return
      2. Purpose of the journey
      3. Name and address of the accommodation
      4. Type of accommodation (hotel, boarding house, or private accommodation)
   4. For **company dinners & lunches**, include in the Cost Commentary:
      1. Names of participants involved
      2. Purpose (e.g., client meeting, lunch, team building, etc.)
4. **ROW employees only** — Notify via email (finance@two.inc) or Slack to confirm that you have submitted a personal expense and seek reimbursement.
5. The Finance Team will then pick it up, assess approval and ensure correct book-keeping and reimbursement procedures.
