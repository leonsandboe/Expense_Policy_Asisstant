# Employee Expenses Policy – Current

Source: https://app.notion.com/p/f66cd0db92874b7e963cefda3b4b9684

This is the current allowance database. Each entry is a specific expense type with its budget, currency, frequency, and audience.

---

## Meals coverage while travelling

- **Audience:** Employees travelling for business
- **Budget per employee / day:** €15 EUR
- **Frequency:** 2x / day
- **Policy:** Company covers up to €15 EUR per meal, per day of travelling — max 2 meals or a €30 EUR total budget per day (if more than 2 meals).

---

## Use of taxis / ubers during business travel

- **Audience:** All employees
- **Budget per employee / day:** €40 EUR
- **Frequency:** Per ride / ad hoc
- **Policy:** This employee expense is not encouraged; however employees can expense such a cost when a) deemed absolutely necessary and/or b) saves time. During expense submission employees need to provide explanation and rationale on why this expense was deemed necessary.

## Office space for employees living in non-hub offices

- **Audience:** All employees
- **Budget per employee / day:** €125 EUR
- **Frequency:** Per day
- **Policy:** Employees are entitled to a company sponsored benefit for co-working space, as long as there are at least 3 FTEs co-located and commit to a minimum usage of co-working space to 3x/week presence in the office.
